Welcome to Backlog Tracker

This website is built to make it easy to keep a backlog of your favourite shows across various streaming services.

The process is simple:

  1. Make an account & sign in
  2. Go to the 'Catalogue' and find a show you want to start tracking
  3. Click on the 'Add Backlog' button and navigate to your Backlog by clicking on your profile at the top-right.
